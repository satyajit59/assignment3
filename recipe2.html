HERBAL TEA

Prep Time: 15 mins Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • fresh herb like giger and chamomile
  • fresh water
  • teapot and strainer

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil 1 cup water.
  2. while boiling water,put herbs into teaspot
  3. pour hot water over herb and cover the teaspot for 15mins.
  4. remove tea bag using tea strainer.
  5. add sweetenersto it and serve it.
