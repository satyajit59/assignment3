Pasta

Prep Time: 20 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 400g Tomato Puree
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • Fresh Basil

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and cooked pasta.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm.
